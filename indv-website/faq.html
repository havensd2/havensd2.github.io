Learn More About Us!

  1. What is the purpose of this website?

    2. This website was made as an opensource knowledgesharing website, we encourage all fans of plants, nature and good food to contribute

  2. What if I dont know enough about plants?

    3. Thats Fine! take your time to explore our website, learn as much as you can and if something interests you learn more about it online or in a book then come back and share what you'ev learned

  3. What do I do if I find one of the edibles I've learned about from your website?

    4. Great! Now we dont want to discourage you but if you decide to eat it, we are not responsible for any temporary or permanent damage caused. We advise you to double and triple check that it is the correct species of edible and then prepare it according to known techniques. If you are even a little unsure don't be too unprepared to throe it out.

  4. How do I contribute?

    5. There are comment sections at the bottom of each edibles profile. Feel free to leave a comment adding detail or information about the edible to its profile. Our people will be alerted. If you have a major edit to contribute or any quality pictures please send it to us through the email found in the Contact Us section found in the navigation bar.
